How to use the App

In order to use the free calculator service provided by the application, click the calculator icon on the bottom of the page and it will open up the calculator interface. Then, enter your height in feet and inches and enter your biological gender. The calculator will show you a to total of four results of what your ideal bodyweight is using four different formulae.
